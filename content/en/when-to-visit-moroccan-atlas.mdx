Short answer: **spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November)** are the best seasons for almost any activity in the Atlas. But the longer answer depends on what you want to do, and the Atlas has options year-round.

---

## By activity

### Day trip to Ourika Valley or Agafay

**Best:** Year-round, with a slight preference for spring (greener valleys, more water in the river) and autumn.

**Summer:** Works well because the valley's coolness contrasts with Marrakech's heat. It's the most popular time.

**Winter:** Works on sunny days. Restaurants may be closed in the higher villages.

**Avoid:** Summer weekends if you want tranquility. The presence of Marrakech visitors can be overwhelming.

---

### Moderate trekking (Imlil, valley circuits)

**Best:** April-June and September-October. Colors are vivid, ideal temperature (15-25°C at medium altitudes), river is flowing.

**July-August:** Valleys are pleasant but can be hot at lower elevations. Medium altitudes (1,500-2,500 m) are cool.

**Winter:** Possible but prepare for short days and below-freezing nights in Imlil.

---

### Toubkal summit (4,167 m)

**Best:** July-September. Snow has cleared from the normal route, weather is stable, views are maximal.

**Spring and autumn:** Possible but there may be snow on the summit and final section. More adventurous but requires more preparation.

**Winter:** Only for experienced mountaineers with snow and ice skills. Crampons and ice axe required.

---

### Tichka pass route

**Best:** Year-round except when there's significant snow (a few weeks per year in winter).

**Winter:** Risk of road closures due to snowfall for 2-4 weeks per year. Check road conditions before leaving.

---

## Summary table

| Activity | Best season | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| Moderate trekking | Apr-Jun, Sep-Oct | Jul-Aug (heat) |
| Toubkal summit | Jul-Sep | Dec-Feb (technical snow) |
| Tichka + Gorges | Mar-Nov | Jan-Feb (snow risk) |
| Ourika Valley | Year-round | Summer weekends |
| Agafay glamping | Sep-May | Jul-Aug (heat) |
