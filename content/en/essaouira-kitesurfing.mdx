Essaouira and its surroundings are one of the world's most consistent kitesurfing destinations. The **Atlantic trade wind** that blows from the northwest between June and August reaches 25-35 knots almost daily — conditions kitesurfers hunt for in the Caribbean and find here, 3 hours from Marrakech, at Moroccan prices.

## Why the wind is so good in Essaouira

Geography explains it: Essaouira sits at a position where the Atlantic trade wind arrives without obstacles from the north. The wind is thermal (developing during the day from the temperature difference between sea and inland) and consistent — without the fluctuations of Mediterranean spots or many Caribbean coasts.

### Wind statistics

- **Windiest months**: June, July, August (25-35 knots average during midday hours)
- **Moderate wind months**: May, September, October (15-25 knots — ideal for beginners)
- **Calmest months**: December, January, February (10-18 knots, with windless days)
- **Direction**: NNW almost always
- **Consistency**: in July, wind suitable for kite is recorded 8 days out of 10

## The best beaches for kitesurfing

### Sidi Kaouki

The **number one spot** in the region. It's 27 km south of Essaouira on the P2101 coast road. A long, wide beach with a very consistent wind window, little current and flat sandy bottom — perfect for learning and for intermediate riders.

Most kite schools are based here. The village has some basic accommodation for multi-day stays.

**Level**: beginners to advanced. **Best season**: May-October.

### Essaouira main beach (north section)

The northern end of Essaouira beach, near the headland, is also a kite spot. Conditions are similar to Sidi Kaouki but there's more shared space with swimmers, surfers and walkers.

**Level**: intermediate to advanced. **Best season**: June-August.

### Moulay Bouzerktoun (Cap Sim area)

About 25 km north of Essaouira. Less known than Sidi Kaouki but very appreciated by locals. Fresher wind than to the south. Access via dirt track — car or taxi needed.

**Level**: intermediate to advanced (currents more variable). **Best season**: June-September.

## Kite schools in Essaouira

The main schools are in Sidi Kaouki. All offer IKO (International Kiteboarding Organization) courses, the international certification standard.

### What to look for in a school

- **IKO certification** for instructors
- **Modern kite equipment** (not more than 3-4 years old)
- Maximum 1:2 instructor-to-student ratio in the water
- Radio communication between instructor and student in the water
- Insurance included in course price

### Approximate prices (2025)

| Course | Hours | Approximate price |
|---|---|---|
| Full initiation | 9-12h | €350-500 |
| IKO Level 1 | 6h | €200-300 |
| Private lessons | 1h | €60-90 |
| Full equipment rental | 1 day | €60-80 |

Prices vary between schools and season. In low season (November-March) there are 20-30% discounts.

## For riders who already know how to kite

If you already have a level and just want to **free ride**, Essaouira is ideal:

- **Equipment rental**: available at Sidi Kaouki schools (~€60-80/day for kite + board + harness)
- **Travelling with your own gear**: CTM buses and taxis accept kite bags with a surcharge
- **Spots by level**:
  - *Beginner/intermediate*: Sidi Kaouki south zone (flat water)
  - *Intermediate*: main beach north (some chop)
  - *Advanced*: Cap Sim, Moulay Bouzerktoun (waves and currents)

## Accommodation for kitesurfers

Most riders staying multiple days base themselves in **Sidi Kaouki** to have the water 2 minutes from accommodation:

- **Auberge de la Plage** (Sidi Kaouki): directly on the beach. Dorms and private rooms. The most used spot by riders.
- **Dar Kenavo** (Sidi Kaouki): surf camp with gear area, hot showers.
- **Essaouira city**: more accommodation and restaurant options. 30 minutes taxi to Sidi Kaouki. For those wanting to combine city and kite.

## Best season by level

| Level | Best months | Expected knots |
|---|---|---|
| Beginner | September-October, May | 15-22 |
| Intermediate | May-June, September | 20-28 |
| Advanced | July-August | 25-35+ |
