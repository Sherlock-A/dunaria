The col du Tichka, at 2,260 meters altitude, is the highest mountain pass in the Atlas and the gateway separating Morocco's green north from the arid south. National road N9 crosses it in 60 km of bends climbing from Marrakech to the pass summit then descending toward Ouarzazate and the desert prelude.

It's one of the most photogenic drives in the entire country.

---

## The Marrakech → Tichka → Ouarzazate route

**Distance:** 190 km (Marrakech → Ouarzazate)
**Duration:** 2h45-3h30 depending on stops and traffic
**Road:** N9 fully paved, wide, generally in good condition

The route first crosses the southern Marrakech plain, then the real ascent begins. The first 30 km after Marrakech are relatively flat. From around km 60, the real switchbacks start: tight bends, rapid elevation changes, landscape shifting from green to ochre to grey stone.

The summit (2,260 m) has a small café and craft vendors. Views from there on a clear day span both north (toward Marrakech and the green Atlas) and south (toward the first desert plains).

---

## Recommended stops

### Taddart (mid-pass area)
Several stalls selling semi-precious stones (minerals or fossils). Prices are negotiable. The landscape here is spectacular: ravines and snow-capped peaks in the background.

### Pass summit (km 90 from Marrakech)
The classic photo stop. The summit café serves mint tea; fossil and mineral vendors are everywhere. Don't linger too long if you're in a hurry, but stop for at least 10 minutes.

### Aït Ben Haddou (detour from Ouarzazate)
Not on the pass itself, but 30 km before Ouarzazate. The ksar (walled city) of Aït Ben Haddou is a UNESCO World Heritage site and the natural set for more films shot in Morocco than anywhere else.

---

## Road conditions

**Summer (June-September):** Perfect. No snow. Heat can be strong in the lower valleys.

**Spring and autumn:** Best for driving. Comfortable temperature, possibility of residual snow only on the highest peaks.

**Winter (December-February):** The pass can close for snow. Not frequently, but it happens. Check road conditions before leaving.

---

## By public transport

CTM and Supratours have daily buses Marrakech-Ouarzazate crossing the Tichka. They're comfortable, with brief stops at the pass. Price: about 80-100 MAD. Journey time by bus is about 3h30.

Shared grand taxis also cover this route with frequent departures from Marrakech bus station.
