Agafay is the answer to a question many people ask: how do you experience the desert without 8-9 hours of driving to Merzouga? The answer isn't perfect (no sand dunes), but it's surprisingly good.

Just 30 km from Marrakech, the Agafay plateau is a mineral landscape completely unlike the gardens and valleys of the Atlas. Grey and beige earth, flat stones, clean horizon, near-total silence and skies with more stars than you'll see from the city.

---

## What Agafay actually is

Agafay is a rocky plateau south of Marrakech, at the foot of the first Atlas foothills. It's not a sand desert but an arid plain with sparse vegetation: scrub, some argan trees, no shade trees.

The landscape resembles the Negev Desert in Israel or certain parts of southern Spain in summer. It's not the Sahara, but it has something the Sahara doesn't: accessibility.

---

## Why visit Agafay

### Stars without the long drive

The most popular Agafay experience is the glamping night: a luxury tent (or cabin) in the middle of the plateau with a Berber dinner and starry skies. What normally requires 2 days of driving is here 40 minutes from Marrakech.

### The sunset

Agafay's magic moment is sunset. The lateral light on the rocky plateau with the Atlas in the background creates a landscape that changes color in minutes: orange, ochre, pink, purple.

### Activities without the crowds

Unlike Merzouga at peak season, Agafay has far less mass tourism. Available activities:
- Quad bikes and motorcycles on the plateau
- Horse riding
- Camel ride (symbolic rather than Saharan)
- Yoga and wellness at the lodges
- Archery
- Stargazing

---

## Where to stay

Agafay has Morocco's most developed glamping offer. The larger camps provide tents with wooden beds, duvets, mirrors and consistent decoration.

Approximate prices per night with dinner and breakfast:
- **Basic glamping:** 600-900 MAD/person
- **Mid-range glamping:** 1,000-1,500 MAD/person
- **Luxury lodge:** 2,000-4,000+ MAD/person

Most camps include a Marrakech transfer or at least precise directions.

## Agafay vs. Merzouga: which to choose?

| | Agafay | Merzouga |
|---|---|---|
| Distance from Marrakech | 30 km | 560 km |
| Landscape | Rocky plateau | Sand dunes |
| Experience | Possible in 1 night | Minimum 2-3 days |
| Price | Higher (premium glamping) | More varied |
| Crowds | Low | High in season |

If the desert is the main goal of your trip, go to Merzouga. If you want a nature and stargazing experience without leaving the Marrakech area, Agafay is ideal.
