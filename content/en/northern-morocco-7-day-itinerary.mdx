One week in northern Morocco is the ideal format to see the imperial cities without rushing and add Chefchaouen, the blue city of the Rif. This itinerary is designed for travelers arriving in Marrakech and leaving from Tangier (or vice versa), combining train, bus and grand taxi.

---

## Itinerary summary

| Day | City | Sleep in |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Arrival Marrakech | Marrakech |
| 2 | Marrakech full day | Marrakech |
| 3 | Train to Fes (via Meknès stop) | Fes |
| 4 | Fes medina | Fes |
| 5 | Volubilis + free afternoon Fes | Fes |
| 6 | Chefchaouen | Chefchaouen |
| 7 | Tangier (ferry or flight) | — |

---

## Day 1-2 — Marrakech

**Day 1 (afternoon):** Arrival, settle into medina riad. Gentle walk through the souks, dinner at Jemaa el Fna.

**Day 2 (full day):** Early morning — Bahia + Saadian Tombs before 9am to avoid groups. Midday — Majorelle Garden. Afternoon — copper and leather souks in the medina. Evening — Jemaa el Fna at its peak.

**Transport to Fes:** Night train (departs ~8pm, arrives ~3am) or day train 7h15. The night train wins if you don't want to lose a visiting day.

---

## Day 3 — Meknès (stop) + Fes

**Morning:** Get off the train in Meknès (~9am). Leave bags in station luggage storage. Visit: Bab Mansour, Moulay Ismail Mausoleum, tea in the medina. 3-4 hours.

**Afternoon:** Train Meknès → Fes (1h). Check in at medina riad. First exploration of Bab Bou Jeloud at dusk.

---

## Day 4 — Fes medina in depth

**7-10am:** Chouara tanneries early (best light, fewer people). Hire an official guide for the first hour to get oriented.

**10am-1pm:** Al-Attarine Medersa + Kairouyine area.

**2-6pm:** Mellah and Al-Andalus quarter (the least tourist-saturated).

**Evening:** Dinner at riad or Rcif restaurants.

---

## Day 5 — Volubilis + free afternoon

**8am:** Grand taxi Fes → Volubilis (30 km, 50 MAD). Visit Roman ruins in 2-3 hours.

**12h30:** Taxi back to Meknès. Lunch in Meknès medina.

**2h30pm:** Train Meknès → Fes (1h). Free afternoon in Fes.

**Alternative:** Use Day 5 for Rabat instead of Volubilis (train Fes→Rabat, 3h).

---

## Day 6 — Chefchaouen

**7am:** CTM bus Fes → Chefchaouen (4h). Arrive at noon.

**Afternoon:** Explore the blue medina, climb to the Spanish mosque for the panorama, have tea at Plaza Uta el-Hammam.

**Evening:** Stay in Chefchaouen. Riads are cheap and the evening atmosphere is relaxed.

---

## Day 7 — Tangier

**8am:** Bus or grand taxi Chefchaouen → Tangier (2-3h). Arrive at noon.

**Afternoon:** Tangier Kasbah and medina, Café Hafa with views of the Strait of Gibraltar, the market in the fishermen's quarter.

**Departure:** Ferry to Algeciras/Tarifa (1-2h) or flight from Tangier Ibn Battouta Airport.

## Estimated budget

| Item | Range/person |
|---|---|
| Accommodation (riads) | 150-350 MAD/night |

| Meals (local) | 80-150 MAD/day |
| Entry fees + Fes guide | 200-300 MAD |
| **Total 7 days** | **~3,500-6,000 MAD (~€350-600)** |
